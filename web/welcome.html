Bioinformatics Services for Structured Analysis and Integration of RNA-Seq experiments

As part of the RNA Bioinformatics center (RBC), which supports all RNA-related research within de.NBI, de.STAIR summarizes the expertise of three participating laboratories to develop and maintain an flexible RNA-Seq analysis workbench. In addition, we offer webinars and training events within the Galaxy universe.

Follow our Project on GitHub

About de.STAIR

de.STAIR develops flexible RNA-Seq analysis workflows for different RNA-Seq protocols, to be used as standalone software, as well as integrated in the Galaxy platform.
Coordinated by Prof. Steve Hoffmann, Prof. Wolfgang Hess, and Prof. Olaf Wolkenhauer, the group is part of the RNA Bioinformatics Center (RBC), which supports all RNA-related research within de.NBI: the German Network for Bioinformatics Infrastructure (ELIXIR Germany).
