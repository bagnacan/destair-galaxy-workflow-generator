Bioinformatics Services for Structured Analysis and Integration of RNA-Seq experiments

As part of the RNA Bioinformatics center (RBC), which supports all RNA-related research within de.NBI, de.STAIR summarizes the expertise of three participating laboratories to develop and maintain an flexible RNA-Seq analysis workbench. In addition, we offer webinars and training events within the Galaxy universe.
